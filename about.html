The Hotel Hermitage is a resort hotel of an international Standard in Sauraha, on the bank of Rapti River, boarding the Chitwan National Park with attractive landscape with big open playgrounds, excellent river side location and largest territory (6 bigha) forest land. Numerous varieties of wildlife including approximately 500 one-horned Rhinoceros (found only in Nepal & India in the world), about 100 Royal Bengal tigers, Leopards, Bears, the dense riverrine and sal jungle the Tharu Villages around it, are all there to give maximum enjoyment to the guests. Extreme care has been taken to maintain a comfortable and hospitable atmosphere. As the name of the hotel suggests, the tranquil and independent life of hermits can be enjoyed in each of the rooms and practically every where inside the hotel compound.
