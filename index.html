Special Facilities

Conference Room

Hotel Hermitage boasts a sophisticated conference hall for your business needs. With meticulous design and state-of-the-art facilities, our conference space provides a professional yet luxurious ambiance. Certified event coordinators ensure a secure environment, offering expert assistance.

Swimming Pool

Hotel Hermitage offers a luxurious swimming pool with crystal-clear, safely maintained waters. Our certified trainers provide expert guidance in the 7-foot-deep pool, ensuring a secure and invigorating experience. Dive into luxury and enjoy the exclusive aquatic amenities at our retreat.

River side view

Experience the enchantment of a beautiful and elegant river view, framed by the lush greenery of a pristine forest. Immerse yourself in the symphony of nature, where wild animals roam freely, birds soar in the open skies, and every moment promises a new adventure. Keep visit keep enjoying nature.

Canoe Riding

The Rapti River coursing through Chitwan National Park offers an ideal setting for canoeing. Seize the opportunity of a lifetime as you paddle along the extensive expanse of the park. Immerse yourself in the spectacle of water birds, observe diverse fish species, and encounter marsh Muggers and Gharial "Crocodiles" up close. Embark on a remarkable journey, navigating the river's currents for an unforgettable experience within the heart of nature.

Bird Watching

Chitwan National Park, boasting a rich avian diversity of over 500 species, transforms into a haven for bird enthusiasts. Winter and early summer welcome migratory birds from Siberia, enhancing the jungle's allure. The months of September to November and February to April provide a thrilling bird-watching spectacle, allowing visitors to witness the park's vibrant ecosystem. Immerse yourself in the breathtaking landscapes as you explore the diverse and captivating world of Chitwan's feathered residents.

Jeep Safari

Embark on an exhilarating Jeep Safari in Chitwan National Park, an optional and seasonal adventure available from November to May. Traverse grasslands, sal forests, and reach Kasara, the park's headquarters. Explore the Gharial Hatchery Farm, gaining insights into crocodile upbringing. Chitwan is renowned for its diverse flora and fauna, including one-horned rhinos, Bengal tigers, Striped Hyenas, and vibrant bird species, offering an unforgettable safari experience.


Jungle walk

During the jungle walk guests are accompained by our trained and well experienced nature guide, where one can experience of scenic view of grassland on the bed of river and dense ferest as well as encounter with wild and aquatic animals which can be unforgettable moments during stay in Sauraha.

Elephant breeding centre

Elephant breeding centre of Chitwan National Park will provide you the opportunity to see baby elephants and ovserve the different material that are used in elephant keeping at elephant training museum set-up inside the breeding centre.

Tharu Culture(Stick Dance) Program

Tharu culture program is one of the unique activities that visitors can enjoy while staying in Sauraha. There are tharu social groups, who show their traditional cultured unique performance & Tharu Cultures with living daily life style, looks amazing.
